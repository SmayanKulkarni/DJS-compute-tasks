This dataset provides information on the size and characteristics of three penguin species (Adélie, Gentoo, and Chinstrap) observed in the Palmer Archipelago, Antarctica.

The dataset is collected by Dr. Kristen Gorman and the Palmer Station, LTER (Long Term Ecological Research) Program.


1) species - The species of the penguin (Adélie, Gentoo, or Chinstrap) - target 
2) island - The name of the island where the penguin was observed (Torgersen, Biscoe, Dream)
3) bill_length_mm - The length of the penguin’s bill (culmen) in millimeters	
4) bill_depth_mm - The depth of the penguin’s bill (culmen) in millimeters
5) flipper_length_mm - The length of the penguin’s flipper in millimeters
6) body_mass_g - The body mass of the penguin in grams
7) sex - The sex of the penguin (male or female)
8) year - The year of observation (2007, 2008, or 2009)
